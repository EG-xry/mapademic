About this map

What is this

8,587,906 OpenAlex authors with 15 or more works, positioned by a coauthorship ForceAtlas2 layout and colored by community. Hue encodes position, so neighboring fields share hues.

Reading the map

Bright splats are highly-cited authors (60,000+ citations). The faint grey web visible at z8-9 is repeat coauthorships - at max zoom the map stays clean, and you see a researcher's coauthorships by searching them or via the info card's Coauthors button. The faint grey speckle in dark areas is background dust - authors with no retained coauthor links, kept searchable but not rendered as interactive dots. The gear's Points setting sizes and colors dots by citations and community at every zoom, not just up close, and can hide the raster splats entirely.

Controls

  • Search finds one researcher and draws rays to their top coauthorships, colored by community membership - a ray's gradient means it crosses between fields.
  • Connect finds the shortest coauthor path between two researchers via the degree-of-separation engine, colored the same way hop to hop.
  • Hover a dot at deep zoom to see the author's name and citations; click it for full details.
  • Share copies a link to the exact view.
  • The gear opens display settings.

Data

Built from an OpenAlex snapshot. See the academic-degree-of-separation project for the path-finding engine.
