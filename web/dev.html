About this map

What is this

8,587,906 OpenAlex authors with 15 or more works, positioned by a coauthorship ForceAtlas2 layout and colored by community. Hue encodes position, so neighboring fields share hues.

Reading the map

Bright splats are highly-cited authors (60,000+ citations). The faint grey web visible at z8-9 is repeat coauthorships - at max zoom the map stays clean, and you see a researcher's coauthorships by searching them or via the info card's Coauthors button. Grey dust is authors without retained coauthor links.

Controls

  • Search finds one researcher and draws rays to their top coauthorships.
  • Connect finds the shortest coauthor path between two researchers via the degree-of-separation engine.
  • Hover a dot at deep zoom to see the author's name and citations; click it for full details.
  • Share copies a link to the exact view.
  • The gear opens display settings.

Data

Built from an OpenAlex snapshot. See the academic-degree-of-separation project for the path-finding engine.
